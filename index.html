All About Deborah

Born in Portland and raised mostly in the Pacific Northwest I am most comfortable in green, rainy climes. However, I do appreciate warmer weather as well, especially since it means dry dogs. I share my home with three canines, and winter means muddy footprints and damp fur in our house.

One thing I enjoy is studying languages. I have studied Greek, Latin, French, and Italian, but please do not ask me to hold a conversation in any of these because I am horribly out of practice. It can be rather difficult to find ways to practice Latin in daily life.
